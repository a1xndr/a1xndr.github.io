Fuzzing QEMU VirtIO Devices: Intro

This summer, I will be contributing to QEMU with funding support from Google Summer of Code 2019. I will be mentored by Bandan Das and Stefan Hajnoczi. I am excited to contribute to one of the most influential open-source projects!

In short, the goal of my work is to develop extensions that will enable efficient fuzzing of QEMU device implementations. I will be focusing primarily on VirtIO devices, due to their popularity and well-defined specification.

I will post updates here, as I make progress
